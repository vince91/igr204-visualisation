IGR204: Happiness in Europe

Welcome to the data Visualisation webpage constructed by Vincent, Ruimin and Zifei!

The dataset used is extracted from Eurofound, containing 9 questions related to European citizens' self-evaluation of satisfaction and happiness, in the European Quality of Life Survey 2012. Questions are:

  • Taking all things together, how happy would you say you are?
  • Satisfaction with economic situation of the country.
  • Satisfaction with social life
  • Satisfaction with health
  • Satisfaction with family life
  • Satisfaction with accommodation
  • Satisfaction with present standard of living
  • Satisfaction with present job
  • Satisfaction with education
